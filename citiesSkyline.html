Cities Skylines

2020-02-01

A Magnicient City Building Game that can be both Realistic and Fantasied

Cities Skylines was a game that I delved in without any expectation and it turned out to be a wonderful simulation. The freedom and the required micro-management was a nice duality, as it gave me a chance to let my creativity run wild while understanding how a city should run. The countless structures and city layouts can make one pour hundreds of hours into this creative game. Although the game was fun and in my opinion the best city building simulation in the market, it was not flawless.


Since the game is mainly focussed on sand box style, I will omit story as a factor.
